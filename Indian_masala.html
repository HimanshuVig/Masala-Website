Welcome To Indian Masala

Indian Masala is a brand of Vimal Masala which has a presence for over 100 years in India. Our brand has been produced 100% natural, authentic and wholesome spices, pastes and many more products that wil give you a flavour of India in every bite. After more than a century of expertise in the spice business Indian Masala is considered to be an essential in kitchens all across India. Our ingredients are hand-picked from the best source and are sorted, grounded by a low temprature grinding process and packages under fully automated conditions maintaining our legacy of delivering the perfect flavours of India.

Indian Masala offers different forms of Indian Spices like masala powders, spice powders etc. You can find a list of all our products on this site along with a wide range of traditional curry recipes such as chicken masala, butter chicken masala, simple chicken curry recipe etc.
